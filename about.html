About-Us

Firdous Charity Organization (FCO)

Non-Governmental Organization that started in 2015 as a community focused initiative and later oﬃcially incorporated on 16th August 2017 under the company’s act No. 1 Of 2012 as a Private Company Limited by Guarantee, under Registration No. 80020000415652. FCO consists of a team of volunteers from different nationalities who decided to dedicate their time and efforts to make a difference in the lives of poor and needy people in disadvantaged communities. We started from Africa with the aim to reach every part of the world. FCO is also registered by the national NGO bureau under number 2459

OUR MISSION

FCO is a non-profit Organization working on developing under-privileged communities through lifelong projects.

OUR VISION

A world where every human being attains their given rights to survival, protection, development, healthy environments and sense of belonging.

Our head oﬃces are strategically located in Ndejje Kibutika, a sub urban region in Wakiso District, Kampala Uganda - East Africa.
